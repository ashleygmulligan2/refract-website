Faye Social House
Fayetteville · Putnam District 331 S Hill Ave 1 parcel · 4 units
A 1929 craftsman in the Putnam District, walkable to everything that makes Fayetteville feel like Fayetteville.
0.7 mi to campus · Razorback Stadium · Bud Walton Arena · Dickson Street · the Greenway · Saturday Farmers Market (running since 1973). Two short-term suites — The Balcony upstairs, The Front Porch on the ground floor — combined on big game weekends as Gameday. The middle floor is a long-term residence (one of ours; we keep someone on site). Tesla charging and a private lot that turns into game day parking on fall Saturdays.
