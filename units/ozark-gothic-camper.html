About this stay

A Black Series HQ19 sited on 2.25 acres, ten minutes from a 236-acre lake.

105 N Betsy Ln is 2.25 wooded acres on the east edge of downtown Rogers — the Ozark Gothic brand spans 5 acres across two parcels here. Quiet enough for a fire pit and the cicadas to be the loudest things, close enough to walk to Lake Atalanta in fifteen minutes. The trailer is a Black Series HQ19 we call The Vortex: a serious off-road rig with a full interior, parked on a level pad with hookups.

Outdoor kitchen under the awning — grill, prep counter, sink, fridge. Inside has a queen bed, dinette that converts, full bath with shower, and Starlink for whatever you need to actually get done. The on-site Cybertruck sits next to the rig for shoot days and atmosphere.

The 2.25 acres include a small course of mini-golf, a fire ring with seating, and clearings under the canopy if you want to set up hammocks. RV hookups are live for guests bringing their own rig — see listing for full specs.

Lake Atalanta is the lake right next door: 236 acres of water, 10 miles of singletrack ringing it, fishing piers, a beach. Connector trails reach the Railyard Bike Park downtown and the Razorback Greenway. Onyx Coffee at The 1907, Honeycomb Kitchen Shop, and the rest of downtown Rogers are five minutes by car. Beaver Lake is fifteen.
