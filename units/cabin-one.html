About this build

A Den Outdoors “Outpost” cabin on 2.25 wooded acres, ten minutes from a 236-acre lake.

105 N Betsy Ln is 2.25 wooded acres on the east edge of downtown Rogers — the same parcel as The Vortex camper. The Ozark Gothic brand spans 5 acres across two parcels here; Cabin One is the first cabin going up on this side.

Walking distance to Lake Atalanta — 236 acres of water, 10 miles of singletrack ringing the lake, fishing piers, a beach. Connector trails reach the Railyard Bike Park downtown and the Razorback Greenway. Onyx Coffee at The 1907 and Honeycomb Kitchen Shop are five minutes by car. Beaver Lake is fifteen.

The design is the Den Outdoors “Outpost” — an efficient, modern, woodland-friendly plan from the Den Outdoors catalog. We’re siting it on the high side of the parcel, with the camper pad and outdoor kitchen continuing to live on the lower clearing.

Sleeping capacity and the booking page are still being finalized. The page will be live for booking when the cabin is photo-ready in early Fall 2026.

Design

Built from the Den Outdoors Outpost plan.

Den Outdoors makes a small catalog of pre-engineered cabin and A-frame plans — efficient footprints, big windows, and a modern-rustic vocabulary that sits naturally in the Ozarks. Cabin One is built from their “Outpost” plan: 325 sq ft, sleeps 2-4, loft bedroom, full bath, mini kitchen, floor-to-ceiling windows.
