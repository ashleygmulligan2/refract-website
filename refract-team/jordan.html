Head of Operations

Jordan Tomblin

If you message a property, this is the person replying. Usually within a few hours, always within a day.

JT

Jordan grew up between Bentonville and the woods at the edge of it. He spends his weekdays answering messages, swapping out smart locks, and figuring out which property needs a new gutter before it rains. He'd rather meet you in person than send a contract back and forth.

He took over day-to-day operations as the family started adding parcels — one weekend at a time — and now runs the rhythm that keeps the four holdings humming. The "Ventures" part of the name isn't posturing; it's a reminder that the next thing always sits a little outside the comfort zone, and somebody has to actually do it.

If you've messaged a property, the reply was probably him. He keeps a list of every guest who's ever stayed, knows which neighbors to wave at when you walk past, and has opinions about which Fayetteville coffee shop has the right amount of light at 7 a.m.

Drives an electric pickup. Makes a competitive Old Fashioned. Believes the best business is one where the same person who picked up the phone also fixes the door.
