JT

Jacey runs the operation that makes every stay feel like the first one. She built the housekeeping system from scratch — checklists, supply runs, vendor partnerships, the small playbook for what counts as “ready” — and now spends a chunk of her week teaching herself how to use AI tools to make the rest of it faster.

She has strong opinions about which sheets last more washes, exactly how a bathroom should smell at check-in, and what time of morning the light at Little Sugar is best for the listing photos. She knows the names of the dogs at every Bella Vista house within walking distance, including the ones that pretend to be tough.

If something at a property is perfect, that's her. If something is wrong, she'll have it fixed before you finish breakfast. She's the reason every short-term suite has the same kitchen drawer organization, the same brand of coffee, and a coaster on the nightstand that you'd actually want to use.

Spends weekends with the dogs at Bella Vista or on the trail at Lake Atalanta. Carries a notebook everywhere. Believes the difference between a property and a home is whether someone cared enough to fold the throw blanket the right way.
