Founder · Design & Hospitality

Ashley Tomblin

The reason Refract feels coherent across four very different holdings.

AT

Ashley named the company. She also designed every property's identity, every photo set, every line of copy on the website — including the one on the home page that says same hands, different light. Before Refract she ran a small design studio in Bentonville, working with the kind of regional brands that built Northwest Arkansas before anyone outside it knew the name.

She handles the front-of-house: the listing photos, the welcome notes, the way each property looks when it shows up in your phone. She has opinions about typefaces. She has opinions about door hardware. She has very strong opinions about which Onyx Coffee location is the best on a Sunday.

The reason Refract feels coherent across four very different holdings is her. She's been the steady hand on the visual side since the first parcel. Still is, even as the kids have taken over most of the day-to-day.

Married Cody in 2008. Mom to Jordan, Jacey, and Cash. Believes the difference between a property and a brand is whether anyone can tell you wrote it.
